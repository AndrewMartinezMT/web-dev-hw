About Me

Hey everyone. My name is Andrew Martinez and I am a Resource Conservation major with a minor in Fire Sciences/Management. This is my first time taking a web design class and so far it has been an awesome learning experience.

I chose the topic of tying flies for this project because I love to fly fish. I started tying my own flies a year ago, taught myself the basics, and now I tie most of my own. It is satisfying to catch a fish on something I made myself. It is also very relaxing to tie flies.

I moved to Missoula for school three years ago and have never thought about going back home. I love living here and I am able to enjoy being outside, usually hunting or fishing. I graduate this semester and haven't been more exicted to finish school and continue working my dream job: a firefighter for the Forest Service.

A great song to fish to.

Where The Green Grass Grows by Tim McGraw
